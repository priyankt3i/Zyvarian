## **4️⃣ Grammar & Syntax**  

### **Word Order (SOV)**
- Zyvarian follows **Subject-Object-Verb** (SOV) structure.  
  - Example: **"I apple eat."** (instead of "I eat an apple.")  
  - Zyvarian: **"Ima tima koro."** (I water drink.)  

### **Verb Conjugation (Prefix System)**
- Verbs use **prefixes** for tense, aspect, and mood.  
  - **Present:** No prefix (e.g., "koro" = drink)  
  - **Past:** **Ta-** (e.g., "takoro" = drank)  
  - **Future:** **Na-** (e.g., "nakoro" = will drink)  

### **Pluralization**
- **Add "-ka"** to pluralize nouns.  
  - Example: **"Tima" (water) → "Timaka" (waters)**  

### **Questions**
- Add **"mo"** at the end of a sentence to make it a question.  
  - Example: **"Ika mo?"** (Is it yes?)
